2.2.28 - Top-down versus bottom-up

For 1000 random doubles
 top-down mergesort is 0.5 times faster than bottom-up mergersort.
For 10000 random doubles
 top-down mergesort is 1.3 times faster than bottom-up mergersort.
For 100000 random doubles
 top-down mergesort is 0.8 times faster than bottom-up mergersort.
For 1000000 random doubles
 top-down mergesort is 1.2 times faster than bottom-up mergersort.
